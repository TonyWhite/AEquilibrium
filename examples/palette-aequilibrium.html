Palette Æquilibrium

The Problem

Text contrast on screens is an important aspect of eye health. However, there is no one-size-fits-all rule:

    • WCAG AAA guidelines are optimized for the visually impaired, but not for the non-visually impaired.
      Excessive contrast of screens and excessive use of screens increases the visual fatigue. It risks creating new visually impaired people.

    • The Solution

    The palette has variants:

    • 1 default variant: alto contrasto, afterimage ridotto, colori più vibranti.
      4 varianti standard contrasto ridotto, conforme a WCAG AA, afterimage ridotto.
      1 HC (High Contrast) variant: altissimo contrasto, conforme a WCAG AAA, per ipovedenti.
      Chromatic equilibrium: tutti i colori con percezione visiva uniforme

    • Features

    • Colori: bianco, nero, rosso, arancio, giallo, verde, ciano, blu, viola, magenta
      Temi: chiaro e scuro per ogni colore
      Conformità: AA per varianti standard, AAA per HC

    • Why?

    Spiega i principi sottostanti:

    • L'affaticamento visivo non è solo "contrasto insufficiente"
      Normovedenti hanno esigenze diverse da ipovedenti
      Accessibilità reale = offrire **scelte**, non imporre uniformità
      La salute oculare a lungo termine conta

    Designed for

    Drastically decrease ocular fatigue.
    Limit the afterimage effect.
    Favors eye relaxation while reading.
    Ocular fatigue decreases when you look away from the screen.

    Variants

    Both Light and Dark has 4 variants.
    · The "High Contrast" has the maximum contrast.
    · The "Default" is the AEQUILIBRIUM between the perceived contrast and the limitation of the afterimage effect.
    · The "Variant 1" has less contrast than "Default".
    · The "Variant 2" has less contrast than "Variant 1".
    · The "Variant 3" has less contrast than "Variant 2".
    · The "Variant 4" has less contrast than "Variant 3".
    · The "High Contrast" of the Dark palette is useful if you like your OLED screen to turn off the black pixels.

    Light features

    A palette for the light there is.
    The "Variant 4" has a slightly gray background, so the screen LEDs will not emit the light with all the available power. You have the perception of white, while your eyes will relax.

    Dark features

    Recommended for long reading on screens. Unlike paper, the screen emits light. A black background with a slightly gray text increase the eye relaxation.
    The "Variant 4" is useful if you like your OLED screen to turn off the black pixels.
